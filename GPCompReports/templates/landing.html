About GPCompReports

GPCompReports provides a comprehensive comparative analysis of residue-residue contacts across {{ total_gpcrs }} human Class A G protein-coupled receptors. Using AlphaFold-predicted structures in both active and inactive conformations, we computed Residue-Residue Contact Scores (RRCS) to identify conformational changes associated with GPCR activation.

Each receptor's contact network was analyzed to identify significant conformational changes, map these changes to transmembrane domains and conserved positions (Ballesteros-Weinstein numbering), and correlate them with human genetic variant data from gnomAD and pathogenicity predictions from AlphaMissense.

The database enables cross-GPCR comparison, identification of Core Functional Residues (CFRs) conserved across the receptor superfamily, and exploration of structure-function-variant relationships at unprecedented scale.
